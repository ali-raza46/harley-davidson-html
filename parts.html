What are Harley-Davidson rims made of?

Excel's Harley rims are constructed of six-series aluminum alloy so they're lightweight and strong, the company says. The deep, lustrous show chrome is guaranteed for one year against flaking and peeling..

How does Harley reserve tank work?

The knob that is present at the left side down under the bike enables us to switch to either MAIN TANK, OFF and the RESERVE TANK. In reserve ,a smaller valve gets opened so it allows the engine to consume less fuel when compared to the main tank.
